Late Work



Objective

Use this form to submit up to 5 late assignments.
Do not email Professor Paulson about late assignments-use this form or see him in-person during class.
Note that points are subtracted for late submissions-generally the later the submission the greater the point loss.

Guidelines:

  • Complete, publish, and submit this form just like any other Summative, Formative, or Project assignment.
  • Multiple submissions are allowed and not penalized, but realize that grading will occur right after the due date.
    This means that being late with this 'Late Work' assignment can mean that you do not obtain any credit for late work.
  • You are responsible to make sure each and every assignment listed here has been completed correctly-this includes web database submissions, D2L file submissions, screenshots are done and combined correctly- follow the instructions in the individual assignment.
    Professor Paulson will not contact you for additional information or clarification.
  • A maximum of 5 late assignments will be accepted, so select the assignments based on impact on your final grade, which is weighted-see D2L for information.
  • The reason each assignment was late must be entered. Failure to include a valid reason means no points awarded-at Professor Paulson's discretion.
  • A final 'Assignment Submissions Report' must be printed as a .pdf file and uploaded to the appropriate D2L Assignment folder.
